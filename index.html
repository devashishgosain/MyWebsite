Devashish Gosain

A nature enthusiast with a deep interest in security and networks. I completed my PhD from IIIT-Delhi in Computer Science (with specialization in network security). Then, I worked as a posdoctoral researcher at INET reserach group, Max Planck Institute of Informatics, Germany with Anja Feldmann.

Presently, I am working as a Postdoctoral researcher at COSIC research group, KU Leuven, Belgium  with Claudia Diaz.
