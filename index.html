Devashish Gosain

A nature enthusiast with a deep interest in networks and security. I completed my PhD from IIIT-Delhi in Computer Science (with specialization in network security) under the supervision of Dr. Sambuddho Chakravarty.  I also collaborated with Dr. H.B. Acharya (Rochester Institute of Technology) during my PhD.
